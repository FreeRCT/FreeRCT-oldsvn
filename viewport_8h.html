enum SpriteOrder

Order of blitting sprites in a single voxel (earlier in the list is sooner).

The number shifted by CS_LENGTH denotes the order, the lower bits are used to denote the kind of sprite being plotted, for mouse-click detection.

See also
PixelFinder, Viewport::ComputeCursorPosition
