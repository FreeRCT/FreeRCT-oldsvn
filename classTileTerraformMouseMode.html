Detailed Description

Tile terraforming mouse mode.

Terraforms an area of the world. It has two modes, a non-zero size, meaning terraforming is limited to that area, or a zero-size (aka 'dot mode'), which means 'unlimited' (the entire world may get changed, if money (and world contents) permits.

The true 'levelling' setting means that the lowest parts are raised or the highest parts are lowered. A false 'levelling' value means that the entire area is moved up or down.
