enum WalkLimit

Limits that exist at the tile.

There are four limits in X direction (NE of tile, low x limit, high x limit, and SW of tile), and four limits in Y direction (NW of tile, low y limit, high y limit, and SE of tile). Low and high is created by means of a random offset from the centre, to prevent all guests from walking at a single line.

Since you can walk the tile in two directions (incrementing x/y or decrementing x/y), the middle limits have a below/above notion as well.
