Detailed Description

Tile definition.

A tile is defined as follows

*      N     North corner is at the top,
*     / \    west corner at the left, and east
*    /   \   corner at the right. South corner
*   W     E  is at the bottom of the tile.
*    \   /
*     \ /
*      S
*
